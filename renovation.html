DR. JOSEPH MONYTUIL WEJANG

Private residence

PROJECTS DETAILS

Year 2022

Location Khartoum, Sudan

Client Dr. Joseph Monytuil Wejang

Team Sudarch & Associates Co. Ltd

Role Design and visualization

Area 500 ㎡

Status Under construction

The concept behind this project is to renovate the house to a modern style villa.


The existing building had problems with spaces layout and proportions. And there was undefine outdoor spaces which were very difficult to be used.


On the other hand, the new proposed design solves those problems by rearrange the spaces for better circulation, and design their dimensions according to the existing module and structure system. Besides, creating an outdoor sitting area with greenery spaces.


Regards the materials, a wide range of well selected sand stone, ceramic and porcelain tiles were applied for internal and external finishes.
