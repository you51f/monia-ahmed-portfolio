Private residence

PROJECTS DETAILS

Year 2020

Location Omdurman, Sudan

Client Mr. Ibrahim Saeed Mohammed

Team Sudarch & Associates Co. Ltd

Role Design and visualization

Area 500 ㎡

Status Complete

A private residence shows facade treatments that corresponding the design concept to reduce the sun radiation impact using louvers and balconies. Also consider the importance of the street view and oriented living spaces and main bedrooms towards it. Applied drainage solutions (dropped manholes) for baths + kitchens.


The building Consists of Ground floor (4 retails + apartment) + 3 Typical apartments floors; 2 apartments per floor. The ground floor occupies by the owner and the rest of the apartments can be rent.

MR. DHEL LOUIS VILLA

Private residence

PROJECTS DETAILS

Year 2022

Location Juba, South Sudan

Client Mr. Dhel Louis

Team Sudarch & Associates Co. Ltd

Role Design, visualization and MEP + structure coordination

Area 1200 ㎡

Status Complete

The client wanted a luxurious level of design, with specific requirements for entertainment spaces (home theater, gym, swimming pool and basketball court). There was an inclined in the plot from east to west. Thus, the design had split levels corresponding the site geometry.


The villa consists of split basement floor (lower and upper level) + ground floor + mezzanine + first floor + terrace floor. With 870 m2 outdoor area and parking at the basement and ground floor level.
